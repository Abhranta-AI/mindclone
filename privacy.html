Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 27, 2026

Mindclone ("we", "our", or "us") is operated by Abhranta AI Private Limited. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use Mindclone at mindclone.studio.

1. Information We Collect

When you sign in with Google, we collect:

  • Your name and email address (from your Google account)
  • Your Google profile picture
  • A unique identifier linked to your Google account

We also collect content you choose to store in Mindclone, including conversations, notes, and memories you create.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your Mindclone account
  • To store and retrieve your personal memories and conversations
  • To authenticate you securely via Google Sign-In
  • To process your subscription and payments
  • To provide customer support

3. Data Privacy — Our Core Promise

Your Mindclone is private. No human at Abhranta AI will ever read your personal entries, memories, or conversations. Your data belongs to you alone, while you are alive. You may choose to transfer it to loved ones as a legacy.

4. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase (Firestore and Firebase Storage), hosted on Google Cloud infrastructure. Data is encrypted in transit and at rest.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties, except:

  • As required by law or legal process
  • With service providers who process data on our behalf (e.g., Firebase, Stripe for payments) under strict confidentiality agreements

6. Data Retention and Deletion

You may delete your account and all associated data at any time from your account settings. Upon deletion, your data is permanently removed from our systems within 30 days.

7. Cookies

We use essential cookies and local storage to maintain your session and app preferences. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies.

8. Children's Privacy

Mindclone is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by email or through the app.
